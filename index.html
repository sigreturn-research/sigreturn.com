Approach

Engineering-grade work, on the things that matter.

Sigreturn Labs focuses on the hard technical problems most security firms don’t touch — the work that lives in debuggers, disassemblers, and CPU manuals rather than in checklists.

We are deliberately small, founder-led, and selective. No compliance theatre, no checkbox audits. We take a limited number of engagements per year so that every one of them gets full attention.
